Effects and Consequences of Cyberbullying




Effects on the Victim

The victim of cyberbullying can experience a significant amount of stress. Some mental effects depression, anxiety, and low self esteem, which may lead to poor academic performance. The victim can also feel powerlessness and isolation due to the harassment in the content of cyberbullying. Some may feel humiliated and embarrassed as a result of this painful event.


Effects on the Cyberbully

The cyberbully can have several consequences for cyberbullying. For example, the bully’s reputation and digital footprint would be affected forever since the cyberbullying is permanently recorded. This will affect the bully’s college applications and employment opportunities. Also, cyberbullying is considered a criminal offense and many laws are against it.
