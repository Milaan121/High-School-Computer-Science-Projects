Steps to Take if You are Cyberbullied




Take These Steps if You are Cyberbullied

  1. Don’t respond to and ignore the cyberbully’s messages
  2. Inform a trusted adult (such as a parent or teacher) about the situation
  3. In addition, talk to friends for moral support (According to the survey, 11 out of 11 peers were willing to help a victim of a cyberbully
  4. Record evidence such as times and descriptions or screenshots of the cyberbullying
  5. Block communication with the cyberbully
  6. Review social media app/website’s terms and conditions
  7. Report cyberbully to social media app/website
  8. Inform and report to school and law enforcement about this event


2 out of 11 people are victims of cyberbullying. It is crucial to stop this and reduce the number significantly. Here are a few steps to take if someone is cyberbullied.
