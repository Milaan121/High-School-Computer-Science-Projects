What is Cyberbullying?

Cyberbullying is bullying that occurs over an electronic device such as cell phones, computers, tablets, etc. This can include sending messages and emails or posting threatening or hurtful messages on social media. The content being shared can be false, negative, or mean and may be public, leading to greater embarrassment for the victim. It message can also be personal and private, leading to humiliation for the victim. The bullying is recorded permanently on the internet and might be public.
